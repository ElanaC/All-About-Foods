American Cuisine

American cuisine follows the taste buds, cooking style, and traditions prepared in the United States. This was originated in Europe and widely influenced by Native Americans, African Americans, Asians, Pacific Islanders, and others. This diversity shows all the history surrounding the United States. Cooking methods of delightful American cuisine has a blend of Native American and early European styles. Typical ingredients found in dishes would include chicken, wheat, corn, and bread. Many holidays like Thanksgiving would not be the same without staples like turkey, mashed potatoes, and various pies. This type of food can be accustomed to all taste buds and are constantly advancing to upgraded levels. American food can be remembered through its diversity with many early cultures effects. Getting together with friends and family, documenting foods through pictures, and watching cooking shows are all commonly found experiences surounding American food.
